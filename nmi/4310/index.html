My Capstone Experience

What I worked on...

My team and I worked for Dan Smigrod at the We Get Around Network, a collection of people interested

in 3D modeling of interior spaces such as homes, cruise ships, etc. In collaboration with Dan,

we created an AR experience for his network which allowed these users to display their 3D models on

their business cards for potential clients.


I developed this app using Unity 3D and the Vuforia Image Target recognition API. These two platforms

allowed me to develop an iOS app, which was submitted to the iOS App Store. To learn more about our

project you can visit our project webpage at wegetaround.mynmi.net

We learned a lot

But also had a lot of fun doing it

What I learned...

I think one of my biggest takeaways from this class was the ability to work with a

team of diverse people. As a Computer Science major, I've always worked in groups

where everyone is a developer. This was the first time I was granted the opportunity

to work with people who had backgrounds in PR, film, design, and so much more. It was a

very humbling experience to say the least. On top of that, it was really cool to see a project

through from start to finish, from alpha to App Store... I've never been able to do something

similar to this, and it's been incredible to do so.


As I go into the job market I know that this will have a lasting impact on how I perceive

people working in other departments. It will definitely grant me the ability to better

communicate with and understand those who don't necessarily have the same background as me.
