Starsiege: Raiders Alpha Trailer


What is Starsiege: Raiders? Starsiege: Raiders is a fast paced looter shooter set in space. Each of the maps are procedurally generated and loadouts are completely customizable. Your goal is to get in, find the artifacts vital for humanity's surival, and escape the planet.

Details

Platform: Currently PC, though console is on the roadmap.

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Contributions:
I had the pleasure of working on Starsiege: Raiders from the inception of the project as a member of the small striketeam that was split off from Draft Gods to begin setting up the base for this new project.

As a result, I was able to gain a lot of insight into how a professional dev team iterates in the early stage of a product. I also was entrusted with setting up many of the core systems for the project including Replication Graph, the Gameplay Ability System, and a variety of other systems vital to our in and out of match loop.
