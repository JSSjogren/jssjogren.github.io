Draft Gods


What was Draft Gods? Draft Gods was a mobile auto chess game based on HiRez Studio's Smite IP. In the game, your goal was to "draft" a roster of your favorite gods and goddesses and improve their stats through combat against AI and other players. This game featured a variety of game modes, both offline and online, prior to being shut down.

Details

Platform: iOS & Android


Role: Gameplay Programmer


Contributions:
My contributions to Draft Gods were largely centered around bug fixes for the first couple of months I worked with Prophecy. As time went on I began working on the creation of various gamemodes that players could queue up for from the home screen.

A significant portion of the development process for Draft Gods involved programming a backend system using Microsoft Azure functions & PlayFab. This was a super interesting difference from most projects I have worked on, as it involved learning C# and more about deploying changes to an Azure function/environment.
